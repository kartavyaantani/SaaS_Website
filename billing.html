Billing FAQs

Get answers to common questions about our billing process.

For monthly plans, you'll be billed on the same date each month. For annual plans, you'll be billed once per year on your subscription anniversary. Usage-based charges are calculated at the end of each billing cycle.
Yes, you can upgrade, downgrade, or change your billing plan at any time from your account settings. Changes to subscription tiers take effect immediately, while billing cycle changes (monthly to annual or vice versa) take effect at the next renewal.
For most resources, you'll continue to have access and will be billed for the overage at the end of your billing cycle. We'll notify you when you reach 80% and 100% of your limits. For subscription plans, you can upgrade to a higher tier to avoid overage charges.
We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for new subscriptions. Beyond that, refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis. For annual subscriptions that are canceled mid-term, we provide prorated refunds for the unused portion minus a small administrative fee.
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and bank transfers. For enterprise clients, we also support purchase orders and invoicing with net-30 payment terms upon credit approval.
