About OsyRix

Discover our story, our mission, and the team behind the innovation

Our Story

Founded in 2015, OsyRix began with a simple idea: create software that makes businesses more efficient and effective. Our founders, Alex Chen and Mia Rodriguez, met while working at a Fortune 500 company and saw firsthand how inefficient software solutions were holding businesses back.

What started as a small team working out of Alex's garage has grown into a global company with over 200 employees serving clients in more than 50 countries. Through the years, we've remained committed to our core values of innovation, excellence, and customer success.

Today, OsyRix is at the forefront of SaaS innovation, combining cutting-edge AI with intuitive design to create solutions that power the world's most ambitious companies.

Our Mission

To empower businesses of all sizes with innovative software solutions that drive efficiency, growth, and competitive advantage. We believe that every organization deserves access to enterprise-grade technology that's intuitive, reliable, and transformative.

We're committed to continuous innovation, ensuring our clients stay ahead in an ever-evolving digital landscape.

Our Vision

To create a future where technology seamlessly enhances human potential. We envision a world where businesses can focus on what they do best, while our solutions handle the rest.

By 2030, we aim to have our technology powering over 1 million businesses worldwide, making enterprise operations more efficient, sustainable, and impactful.

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to industry leadership

2015

OsyRix was founded by Alex Chen and Mia Rodriguez in Silicon Valley. The first prototype of our core platform was developed with just 5 team members.

2017

Secured $5M in Series A funding. Released our first major product, OsyRix Core, and expanded the team to 25 employees. Opened our first international office in London.

2019

Reached 100 employees and 500 clients worldwide. Launched OsyRix Analytics with industry-first AI capabilities. Awarded "Most Innovative SaaS Solution" at the Global Tech Awards.

2021

Secured $30M in Series B funding. Expanded to over 150 employees serving clients in 30+ countries. Launched our mobile application suite.

2023

Celebrated reaching 1000+ clients and 200+ employees globally. Opened offices in Singapore and Sydney. Introduced groundbreaking AI-powered workflow automation features.
